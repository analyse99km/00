ZENO ORGANISM: DEEP AUDIT EXTRACTION WORKFLOW

WHAT IT WILL DO (AND WHAT IT EXTRACTS):
Zeno operates as a high-volume, deep-audit intelligence gatherer based on your configuration (currently targeting "crypto startups"). 

1. Target Discovery & Quota Routing
It constantly searches for high-signal discussions regarding the target topic. It builds a queue of accounts/startups discussing the topic until it hits a mandatory minimum quota (e.g., 100 accounts). 

2. Deep Account Dossier Extraction
Once it selects a target account, Zeno does a "Deep Audit". It extracts massive amounts of contextual data to build a complete dossier:
- Profile Intelligence: It extracts the account's full profile details and summary.
- Historical Posts: It extracts up to 100 public posts from the account (including the raw text, hashtags, cashtags, URLs, and media attachments).
- Audience Engagement (Comments): For every single post it pulls, it also extracts up to 50 public comments and replies left by other users, giving you a map of the community sentiment.
- Traction Metrics: It records the exact number of likes, retweets, quotes, replies, views, and bookmarks so you can see how viral the startup is.

3. Infinite Discovery (No Repeats)
Every single account it fully audits is permanently memorized in its database. When Zeno runs again tomorrow (or a month from now), it will instantly skip any account it already built a dossier for. It strictly focuses on discovering NEW startups.

WHERE IT SAVES THE DATA:
1. The Local Dossier Vault (`data/deep_audit/`)
Instead of dumping data randomly, Zeno builds a highly organized intelligence folder on its local machine:
- It creates global databases (`accounts.jsonl`, `posts.jsonl`, `comments.jsonl`, and `metric_snapshots.jsonl`) so you can search across all startups at once.
- It also creates dedicated folders for every single startup (e.g., `data/deep_audit/accounts/<handle>/`) containing isolated copies of their specific posts, profiles, and comments. 
- It generates a human-readable `summary.md` detailing the entire operation.

2. The Permanent Target Repository
After the deep audit is 100% complete and the dossiers are built, Zeno immediately takes the entire `data/deep_audit/` folder and pushes it to your permanent vault:
https://github.com/analyse99km/project1-crypto_startups

Once the data is safely secured in your target repository, Zeno goes to sleep until its next run.
